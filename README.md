# Simpletype Editor Pro

> A distraction-free, single-file text editing environment.
> *Designed by Tobias Schmidt Services LLC.*

Simpletype Editor Pro is a lightweight, browser-based writing tool designed for focus and speed. It combines a minimal interface with robust local persistence, ensuring your words are safe without requiring accounts, clouds, or complex installations.

## Features

### Focus-First Design
The interface is stripped of clutter, utilizing the **Inter** typeface for optimal readability. The responsive layout adapts seamlessly to any window size.

### Persistent Auto-Save
Never lose your work. The editor automatically saves your content to your browser's local storage as you type, restoring it instantly when you return.

### Adaptive Theme
Switch effortlessly between a bright, clean Light Mode and a high-contrast Dark Mode designed for low-light environments. The application remembers your preference between sessions.

### Live Statistics
Track your progress with real-time word and character counts located in the status bar.

### Instant Export
Download your work immediately as a standard `.txt` file with a single click.

## Getting Started

1. Download `Simpletype Editor Pro.html`.
2. Open the file in Safari, Chrome, or Firefox.
3. Begin writing.

---

## Privacy & Security

**Simpletype Editor Pro** operates entirely within your browser. Your text is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.
